Context-Aware Legal Clause Verification

Problem

Legal contracts often hide risky or unfair clauses in complex language, making them hard to understand for non-experts.

I built an AI system using LegalBERT that automatically classifies contract clauses, flags legal risks, and explains decisions using model interpretability techniques like SHAP.

The system focuses on Indian Contract Law and highlights both legal and social context, making it useful for real-world review.
